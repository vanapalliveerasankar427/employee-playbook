CONFLICT RISK RADAR

See conflict building before it costs you

Conflict rarely “starts” in the meeting. It accumulates in blind spots: misaligned incentives, unresolved history, pressure spikes, and communication drift. Use this to spot the conditions, quantify the risk, and choose the right level of response.
Explore Categories
Clarity, Priorities & Direction Manager Relationship & Leadership Style Workplace Dynamics & Politics

When to Use This Radar

  • When a relationship feels “off” but you can’t prove anything yet
  • Before a difficult conversation (feedback, boundaries, delivery risk, performance)
  • When stakeholders are split and you’re being pulled into the middle
  • When pressure, ambiguity, or politics are making people sharp
  • When you need to protect your reputation while still delivering outcomes

Pro Tips

  • Rate the environment (conditions), not who you wish people were
  • High risk doesn’t mean “fight” — it means choose the right containment
  • Look for clusters: one high spike is manageable; a pattern is a forecast
  • Re-run after any deadline change, leadership shift, or public disagreement
  • Use the output as a private plan — not something to argue with the other person about
