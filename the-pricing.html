Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

No trial. Essentials is free and gives you a starting point; paid tiers unlock the depth and the strategic tools.

Can I cancel?

Yes — with 30 days’ notice. You keep access during the notice period so you can close loops properly.

Which tier should I choose?

Core is the best all-rounder. Strategic Edge is for Playbooks and higher-leverage strategy when the stakes rise.

How often is the Edge Briefing?

Monthly — concise, practical, and focused on what changes outcomes fastest.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes. Switch to annual to lock in the lower rate (£88 for Core, £160 for Strategic Edge).

Is this legal advice?

No. It’s practical workplace strategy to help you think clearly and act calmly. For legal advice, speak to a qualified professional.
