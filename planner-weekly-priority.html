WEEKLY PRIORITY PLANNER

Turn a busy week into a controlled week without letting “urgent” decide your agenda

This planner forces a clean weekly truth: what matters, what fits, and what gets parked. You’ll set 1–3 outcomes for the week, score tasks with a simple priority signal, and drag them into the right day so your week stops leaking into evenings.

Then generate a clean weekly plan and a short boundary script to defend your top outcomes.
Related categories
Clarity, Priorities & Direction Expectation, Burnout & Capacity

When to Use This

  • When your “weekly plan” is just a list that grows and never finishes.
  • When priorities change mid-week and you need a simple trade-off rule.
  • When meetings eat the week and the real work ends up after-hours.
  • When you need a plan you can share without oversharing.
  • When you want a calmer week without sacrificing outcomes.
If it can’t fit in the week, it’s not a plan. It’s a wish.

How to Use It (fast)

  • Pick 1–3 outcomes that define a “good week”.
  • Add tasks as verb + deliverable (not “work on X”).
  • Score tasks with Impact, Urgency, Effort to reveal what actually wins.
  • Drag tasks into the right day. Keep Monday realistic and Friday protected.
  • Generate a weekly plan + boundary script to keep expectations clean.
