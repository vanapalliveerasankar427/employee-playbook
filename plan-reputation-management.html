REPUTATION MANAGEMENT PLAN

Protect your credibility, shape the story, and stay unshakeable — even in messy workplaces

This plan helps you manage your professional reputation deliberately: what you are known for, how you are perceived, where you are exposed, and how to correct the narrative without becoming reactive. Draft your inputs, then generate a Reputation Pack: a one-page positioning plan, high-risk reputation traps to avoid, and scripts for corrections, clarity, and confidence.

When to Use This Plan

  • When you suspect your work is being misrepresented or selectively reported.
  • When you are in a “high visibility” zone (promotion, restructure, performance noise, leadership change).
  • When you are labelled unfairly (difficult, slow, not strategic, too blunt, too quiet) and it is sticking.
  • When someone else is taking credit, shifting blame, or controlling the narrative around delivery.
  • When you want to build a reputation that travels ahead of you — without self-promotion cringe.

How to Use It (fast)

  • Assume there is always a story running. Your job is to make sure it is accurate and useful.
  • Separate facts (evidence) from interpretations (opinions). You can control facts.
  • Use “receipts”: written updates, decision logs, clear ownership, and follow-ups.
  • Protect tone. Calm clarity beats heat. A controlled response is a reputation signal.
  • This is not about pretending. It is about making your value unmistakable and defensible.
